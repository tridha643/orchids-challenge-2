Territorial Acknowledgement
The University of Alberta, its buildings, labs and research stations are primarily located on the traditional territory of Cree, Blackfoot, Métis, Nakota Sioux, Iroquois, Dene, Inuit, and many others whose histories, languages, and cultures continue to influence our vibrant community.
